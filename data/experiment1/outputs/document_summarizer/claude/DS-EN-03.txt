### Key Points
This extract from a Memorandum of Understanding signed on 14 January 2024 between Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT) and the National University of Singapore (NUS) establishes a joint research and education programme covering doctoral co-supervision, shared curriculum development for an AI Engineering Certificate, reciprocal faculty exchanges, and coordinated external funding applications.
