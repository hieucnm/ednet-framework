Prof. Sarah Chen noted that if the third HUST application is received, the total cohort would reach eleven, and she committed to confirming this with the NUS international office and sending a formal update to all attendees by Monday.

The scheduling of the mid-year review was discussed. Assoc. Prof. Tran Minh Quang noted that a June meeting in Hanoi had previously been discussed. Dr. Ha Duc Thinh confirmed that HUST could host the event on either June 14th or June 15th, with conference facilities available on both days. June 14th was tentatively selected, and Assoc. Prof. Tran Minh Quang indicated he would issue a calendar invite following the call.
