## Collaboration Framework Outline
**Parties:** Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), National University of Singapore (NUS)
**Purpose:** Establish a structured bilateral framework for joint postgraduate research supervision, faculty exchange, and co-development of advanced AI and semiconductor engineering curriculum within the VASTN consortium.
**Effective period:** 5 years from date of signing, with annual review

### Scope of Collaboration
The collaboration will focus on joint supervision of doctoral and master's research students, reciprocal short-term faculty exchanges, co-development of curriculum modules for the VASTN joint certificate programme, joint applications for external research funding, and hosting an annual joint research seminar.
