## Collaboration Framework Outline
**Parties:** HCMUT, HUST, NUS, Intel Vietnam, Bosch Vietnam  
**Purpose:** Establish a multi-party knowledge transfer and workforce development framework enabling systematic sharing of industry expertise, research capabilities, and training resources across the VASTN network, in support of Vietnam's national strategy for AI and semiconductor talent development.  
**Effective period:** 3 years, with a mid-term review at 18 months and renewal option subject to steering committee approval.

### Scope of Collaboration
The collaboration will focus on conducting annual joint workforce needs assessments to identify skill gaps, co-developing and delivering short courses with joint certification, implementing an industry mentorship program, sharing licensed software tools, and organizing an annual VASTN Innovation Challenge.
