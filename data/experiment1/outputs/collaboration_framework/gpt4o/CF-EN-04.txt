## Collaboration Framework Outline
**Parties:** Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), RMIT University Vietnam  
**Purpose:** Establish a short-term student mobility arrangement allowing final-year undergraduate students from each institution to complete elective modules at the partner institution, with mutual credit recognition, in support of the VASTN interdisciplinary education objectives.  
**Effective period:** 2 academic years (2024–2025 and 2025–2026), renewable by mutual agreement

### Scope of Collaboration
The collaboration involves reciprocal acceptance of up to 10 students per semester from each institution for elective module enrolment. Eligible modules will be drawn from a jointly agreed list reviewed annually. The collaboration includes credit recognition of up to 9 credit units per semester at HCMUT and the equivalent at RMIT Vietnam. A joint orientation session for incoming exchange students will be conducted at the start of each semester, and an end-of-programme review meeting will be held between student affairs coordinators from both institutions.
