### Key Points
The AUN-QA Framework Version 4.0 is a revised set of quality assurance standards for Southeast Asian universities. It focuses on Programme Outcomes and Curriculum Design, Teaching and Learning Approach, and Programme Quality Enhancement. Institutions must align learning outcomes with national and ASEAN qualifications frameworks, with at least 85% of programme outcomes mapped to course outcomes. From 2025, engineering and technology programmes must include 160 hours of industry-engaged learning, with up to 40% virtual engagement. Programmes must conduct annual reviews with external representatives and report on graduate employment and student satisfaction.

### Implications for the Network
Member institutions must adapt to stricter evidence requirements and prepare for a transition to Version 4.0 standards by their next renewal between 2025 and 2027.
