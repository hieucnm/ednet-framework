### Agenda Items Discussed
The meeting commenced with a review of student performance outcomes for Semester 1. Two students did not complete the semester due to visa issues and academic discontinuation. The machine learning theory module saw the strongest performance, while the embedded systems lab was identified as the weakest, with five students needing supplementary assessment. An equipment disparity at HUST was noted as a contributing factor, with plans for remediation before Semester 2.

The curriculum revision proposals were discussed, including the introduction of a four-hour workshop on responsible AI and ethics, and restructuring the capstone project to a team format. NUS highlighted the need for updated assessment rubrics for the team capstone.
